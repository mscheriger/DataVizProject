Welcome to our data visualization comparing taxi and rideshare use in Chicago!

The following charts will display different views to help you explore & understand how taxi and rideshare use varies across Chicago. Our data is adapted from the Chicago Data Portal's Rideshare and Taxi datasets. The data is from October 2019.
